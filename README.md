	- Brienz to Brienz Rothorn Bahn
		The Rothorn Bahn station is directly across the road from the main SBB station — just cross the street.
		eight departures daily starting around 8:30 AM
		50CHF with travel pass 
		add 8 CHF for seat guarantee (strongly recommended)
		takes 1 hour

	  - bring a jacket — it's cool even in summer.
